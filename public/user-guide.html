Role Overview

Who sees what on Dollar V2

👤
Agent
Sees own target, pipeline, achieved amount & commission. No team data.
🧑‍💼
Manager
Assigns targets to agents & self, views team leaderboard & team commissions.
🏢
VH
Assigns targets to managers, sees all managers leaderboard, drills into teams.
🎯
Sales Head
Full visibility across all VHs, managers and agents. Same views as VH.
📞
PreSales
Sees own calls & sales summary. Separate dashboard without revenue targets.
⚙️
Admin
Full access. Can impersonate any user to see their exact view.
🔐

Logging In

Accessing the platform

  • 1
    Open the app in your browser Use the link shared by your admin. Works on mobile and desktop.
  • 2
    Click "Sign in with Google" Use your company Google account (the email your admin registered you with).
  • 3
    You land on your Dashboard The view is personalised to your role automatically — no extra setup needed.
⚠️ If you see "No data found", make sure you're logged in with the exact email your admin used when adding you. Contact your admin if you're unsure.

Agent Guide

Everything you need to track your own performance

Pages you have access to

🏠 Dashboard 📊 My Deals

Dashboard — What you'll see

🎯
Monthly Target
Your revenue target for the month set by your manager.
✅
Achieved
Total realised (paid) revenue from your deals this month.
📈
Sale Value (Pipeline)
All deals in pipeline — includes unpaid deals too.
💵
Commission
Money you've earned so far based on slab rules.
📊
Achievement %
How close you are to hitting your target.
🔮
Projected %
If all pipeline deals close, what % you'll achieve.

How to read your commission

  • 1
    Commission is slab-based You earn a % based on how much of the target you've achieved. Higher slabs = higher %.
  • 2
    Look at the "Performance Snapshot" section Shows Target, Achieved, Gap remaining, and Projected — all in one place.
  • 3
    Check "₹X to go" This tells you exactly how much more revenue you need to close the gap.
💡 Your deals are synced from your CRM. You don't need to enter anything manually — just focus on closing deals and the numbers update automatically.

Manager Guide

Assigning targets, tracking your team & managing commissions

Pages you have access to

🏠 Dashboard 🎯 Assign Targets 📋 My Targets 👥 My Team

Step 1 — Assign targets to your agents

  • 1
    Go to "Assign Targets" from the menu You'll see a list of your team members (agents) on the left side.
  • 2
    Click on an agent's name Their target form opens. You'll see one card per program (e.g. Dollar Gold, Dollar Plus).
  • 3
    Set the Revenue Target Enter the monthly revenue target for that agent in the "Revenue Target" field.
  • 4
    Set the commission slabs Add slab rows: each row has an "Up to ₹X" threshold and a "% commission" rate. Example: Up to ₹5,00,000 → 3%, Up to ₹10,00,000 → 5%.
  • 5
    Click "Assign Target" A green confirmation appears. The agent will see their target on their dashboard immediately.
⚠️ You need to assign targets separately for each program. If an agent works across two programs, set targets for both.

Step 2 — Add your own Personal Contribution (optional)

  • 1
    Scroll to the Program section (same Assign Targets page) At the top you'll see your own program target cards — separate from your agents.
  • 2
    Enter "Team Contribution" amount This is your own personal revenue target that adds to the team total. For example, if you personally carry ₹8L, enter 800000.
  • 3
    Click "Assign Target" Your personal contribution is saved and will now appear in your Dashboard's Team Target.
💡 Your own commission is based on team goals — not personal targets. The "Team Contribution" field just ensures the team target number on the Dashboard is accurate.

Step 3 — Reading your Dashboard

🎯
Team Target
Sum of all agent targets + your personal contribution.
📈
Team Sale Value
All deals in your team's pipeline this month.
✅
Team Achieved
Realised (paid) revenue across your whole team.
💵
Total Money Made
Your commission + any T2 bonus earned.
📊
Achievement %
Team Achieved ÷ Team Target × 100.
🔮
Projected %
If all pipeline deals close, what % you'd achieve.

Step 4 — View your own assigned targets (My Targets)

  • 1
    Go to "My Targets" from the menu This shows the program targets your VH / Sales Head has assigned to you.
  • 2
    Check each program card See the slab structure, your Team Sale Value, Team Achieved, and commission earned per program.
  • 3
    Check the "partial" badge A yellow "partial" badge means your team hasn't reached the threshold for that slab yet — you're earning a lower rate.

Step 5 — View and filter your team (My Team)

  • 1
    Go to "My Team" from the menu See all your direct reports and their details in one place.
  • 2
    Use "Direct Reports" or "Full Team" tabs "Direct Reports" shows only your immediate team. "Full Team" shows nested team members too.
  • 3
    Filter by role or search by name Use the role pills (All / Manager / Agent / Pre-Sales) or the search bar to find specific members.

VH & Sales Head Guide

Managing managers, targets and org-wide visibility

Pages you have access to

🏠 Dashboard 🎯 Assign Targets 📋 My Targets 👥 My Team

Assigning targets to managers

  • 1
    Go to "Assign Targets" You'll see a list of managers under you. Click a manager's name to open their target form.
  • 2
    Set slabs per program Each program card has a slab table. The manager's commission is calculated against their team's total performance on that program.
  • 3
    Save — the manager sees it under "My Targets" The assigned target shows up on the manager's "My Targets" page instantly.

Dashboard — Managers leaderboard

  • 1
    Your Dashboard shows a Managers Leaderboard Each row = one manager with their Team Target, Sale Value, Achieved, and commission.
  • 2
    Click "Drill In" on any manager row You drop into that manager's team and see their agents' leaderboard — same as the manager sees it.
  • 3
    Use the breadcrumb to go back Click the back arrow or breadcrumb at the top to return to the top-level managers view.
💡 Sales Heads can see all VHs and drill all the way down to individual agents — full org visibility in a few clicks.

Switching Months

How to view past or future months

  • 1
    Find the month selector at the top of any page It shows the current month (e.g. "Apr 2026") by default.
  • 2
    Click the left / right arrows to navigate Move backward to see last month's performance or targets, or forward to plan ahead.
  • 3
    All pages reflect the selected month Dashboard, leaderboard, My Targets — everything updates to show data for the selected month.
⚠️ If you assign targets for a future month, switch to that month first before clicking "Assign Target".

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

My dashboard shows ₹0 — what's wrong?
Either your manager hasn't assigned a target yet, or your login email doesn't match the email in the system. Contact your manager or admin.
Why is my commission showing as partial?
A "partial" commission means your achieved amount is within a slab range but the full slab unlock threshold hasn't been crossed yet. Close more deals to jump to the next slab.
I assigned a target but the agent can't see it
Ask the agent to refresh their browser. Data syncs in real-time but a hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R) clears the cache.
Can I assign targets for next month already?
Yes — switch to next month using the month selector at the top, then assign targets normally. They'll go live when that month begins.
Team Target on my Dashboard looks lower than expected
Make sure you've filled in your own "Team Contribution" in Assign Targets (your personal quota). Agent targets alone don't include your own contribution.
How often does data refresh?
Deal data syncs from the CRM automatically. Dashboard data refreshes every time you load the page or switch months. You can also force a refresh using the 🔄 icon.
I made a mistake in the slabs — can I re-assign?
Yes. Go to Assign Targets, select the same agent and month, update the slabs, and click "Assign Target" again. It overwrites the previous assignment.
